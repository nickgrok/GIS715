ALLUVIAL DIAGRAM - CLASSMATE DATA

An Alluvial Diagram was generated using the class visualization data

This data was visualized to show relationships between our classmates places of birth, whether they are a cat or dog lover, and their favorite number. This data shows the largest group of classmates as dog people, but no real relationship between favorite number and what animal they like best.
